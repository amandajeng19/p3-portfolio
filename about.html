About Me

Hi, I’m Amanda!

I’m an undergraduate design student at SFU’s School of Interactive Arts & Technology. I recently completed a 12-month co-op as a Digital Communications Assistant at the Department of Fisheries and Oceans.

My focus is in graphic and UX/UI design, but I’m also interested in psychology and education. I’d love to explore the intersection of these industries, and improve my design practice by applying knowledge of how our brains learn and process information.
